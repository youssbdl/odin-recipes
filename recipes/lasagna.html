Lasagna

Description

Layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and perfectly cooked pasta come together in this timeless lasagna recipe. Packed with hearty flavors and cheesy goodness, this comforting Italian dish is perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or make-ahead meals. Whether you're serving guests or treating yourself, this homemade lasagna delivers a satisfying bite every time. Simple ingredients, traditional methods, and a whole lot of love—this is lasagna the way it was meant to be.

Ingredients

    The meat sauce :
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or half beef, half pork)
  • 1 can (400g / 14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
      • For the béchamel sauce :
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 3 cups (750ml) milk (preferably warm)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • A pinch of ground nutmeg
      • Other Ingredients
  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (fresh or dry)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

    • Steps

      I. Make the meat Sauce :
    1. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Add the chopped onion and sauté until soft (about 5 minutes).
    3. Stir in the garlic and cook for 1 minute.
    4. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
    5. Drain excess fat if needed.
    6. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar.
    7. Reduce heat and let simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
      II. Make the bechamel sauce :
    1. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in the flour and cook for about 1-2 minutes to form a roux.
    3. Gradually whisk in warm milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
    4. Continue cooking and stirring until the sauce thickens (about 5-7 minutes).
    5. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Remove from heat.
      III. Prepare the pasta :
    1. If using dry lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
    2. If using fresh lasagna sheets, they can typically be used directly.
      IV. Assemble the lasagna :
    1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. In a large baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    3. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
    4. Spread some béchamel sauce over the noodles, then add more meat sauce.
    5. Sprinkle with mozzarella and a bit of Parmesan.
    6. Repeat layers (noodles, béchamel, meat sauce, cheese) until ingredients are used up.
    7. Finish with béchamel and a generous layer of cheese on top.
      V. Bake :
    1. Cover the dish loosely with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove foil and bake uncovered for an additional 15-20 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbling.
    3. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing.

    Enjoy!
